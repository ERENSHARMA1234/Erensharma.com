Information

Cristiano Ronaldo was born on 5 February 1985. His full name is Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro. He started playing soccer at the age of eight. Ronaldo was expelled out of his school at the age of fourteen because of throwing a chair on his teacher who, Ronaldo thought, disrespected him. Then, he saw soccer as his career. In the year 2003, Manchester United bought him for $100 Million. He was selected as the captain of Portugal national soccer team in 2008. In the year 2009, Real Madrid bought him and in the year 2018,Juventus bought him. He played again for Man Utd in year 2021. He is currently playing for Al Nassr.
